Scope and editorial policy

APPLIED ECONOMICS is a quarterly publication of the Department of Economics, Faculty of Economics, Management and Accounting of Ribeirão Preto, University of São Paulo, with an editorial line dedicated exclusively to scientific articles in applied economics. Its focus is to publish works of economic analysis applied to specific problems of interest to both public and private sectors, and containing, in particular, quantitative studies, whose results can bring the theory to reality. The interaction between empirical work and theory, analysis and economic policy is therefore a major differentiator of the magazine. The journal Applied Economics arises as an open space for the entire academic community, nationally and internationally.
Articles submitted are evaluated initially by the Editors regarding their suitability for the Journal editorial. Once accepted the submission, the article is sent for evaluation by referees nominated by the editors at sitema double-blind trial.

 

Preparation of originals

Articles submitted for assessment must meet the following standards:


  • Provide unprecedented in the country and abroad.
  • At first page should contain the article title, abstract with up to 100 words in Portuguese and English keywords in Portuguese and English and JEL classification.
  • In version "evaluation", just a file in pdf format with all equations, tables, and figures in the body text and reference at the end. Should not appear in this version the name of the authors.
  • If the article be accepted for publication, the magazine's staff will contact the editors for publishing activities. At that time will be required the following files:
    • LATEX source file format
      Bibliographic reference file in BibTex standard
      File with figures in TIFF or EPS Spreadsheet with all tables.
