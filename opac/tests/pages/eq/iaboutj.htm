Basic information

Published since 1976, the journal Eclética Química aims to publish original research contributions covering all aspects of Chemistry.

The journal is a quarterly publication of the Universidade Estadual Paulista Júlio de Mesquita Filho - UNESP.

Its abbreviated title is Eclet. Quím., which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes and bibliographical references and strips.

 

Indexing sources

The articles published in Eclética Química are indexed by:

  • Abstracts Journal (Referatvni Zhurnal)
  • CAB Abstracts
  • Chemical Abstracts
  • SciFinder Scholar
  • Red Alyc
