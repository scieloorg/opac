Basic information

Educar em Revista is a bimonthly publication (online) from Setor de Educação or Universidade Federal do Paraná, dedicated to the publishing of original scientific works in Portuguese, Spanish, French or English, on Education. Texts should be submitted to Educar em Revista by SER - Sistema Eletrônico de Revistas ("Electronic Journals System") at: <http://revistas.ufpr.br/educar>. Select "Cadastro" ("Register").

Educar em Revista was named Revista de Educação in the 1977 and 1978. Its two issues are available in:
1978 - http://ojs.c3sl.ufpr.br/ojs2/index.php/educar/issue/view/1556
1977 - http://ojs.c3sl.ufpr.br/ojs2/index.php/educar/issue/view/1555

The journal's title abbreviation is Educ. rev., that must be used in bibliographic lists, footnotes, references and bibliographic labels.
