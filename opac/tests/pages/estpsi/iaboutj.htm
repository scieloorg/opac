Basic information

Estudos de Psicologia (Campinas) is a quarterly journal of the Graduate Program of Psychology of the Center of Health Sciences of the Pontifical Catholic University of Campinas. Founded in 1983, it welcomes contributions from the national and international scientific communities.

Our mission is to promote and disclose scientific and technical knowledge in the area of Psychology, to discuss the meaning of practices in professional and research fields by publishing original articles that represent relevant contributions to Psychology. It also publishes theoretical works, reviews, and communications relevant to Psychology as a science and profession.

The journal was founded in 1983 and currently is ranked as A1 from the Qualis/Capes list (http://www.capes.gov.br/avalicao/qualis). The journal is indexed in national and international data bases: Clase, Doaj, Index Psi, Latindex, Lilacs, PsycINFO, Psicodoc, RedaLyc, Scopus e SciELO.

Estudos de Psicologia stimulates the contributions of national and international scientific community. All items must be submitted electronically, by page ScholarOne via the link: <http://mc04.manuscriptcentral.com/estpsi-scielo>.

Estudos de Psicologia does not charge submission and/or publication fees.

The journal uses a tool for detecting plagiarism on all articles before submitting them to peer review.

Estudos de Psicologia is abbreviated as Estud. psicol. (Campinas), which should be used in bibliographies, references, footnotes, and reference captions.
