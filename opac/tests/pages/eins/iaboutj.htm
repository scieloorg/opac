Basic Information

einstein (São Paulo) is an official publication for scientific dissemination of the Instituto Israelita de Ensino e Pesquisa Albert Einstein, ISSN 1679-4508, quarterly published and designed to register the production and contributions of the health scientific community.

The journal title abbreviation is einstein (São Paulo), which should be used in bibliographies, footnotes, references and legends.

All processes are electronically conducted and can be accessed at http://apps.einstein.br/revista

einstein (São Paulo) is an open access journal, and there is no charge for submission, review, translation and publication of articles.
