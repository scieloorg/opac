Basic information

Objective:
The journal Ensaio: Assessment and Public Policies in Education aims to publish articles related to Education, focusing on evaluation and public policies in education, prioritizing the results of research, theoretical studies and essays.

Periodicity:
Quarterly

Areas of interest: 
Humanities, sub-area: education
