SUBSCRIPTION

 

Subscription is free.

In order to receive the printed journal, in a systemic way, it is necessary for the institution to make the request through the email ensaio@cesgranrio.org.br, writing in the subject Subscription request and, in the body of the message: name of the institution; CNPJ; Address, phone number (with country code), and name, position and sector of the responsible for receiving.

The request for subscription cancellation can be made through the same email.

Note: the subscription request will be granted as long as it does not exceed the circulation of 1.500 copies and its reservations.
